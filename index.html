Project Description

"In Search of Landscapes" is a curated collection of landscape photos taken on a Google Pixel6 phone. This collection explores a different approach to data curation. Instead of selecting images based on my perception of the best "landscape" aesthetic, I gathered images from the perspective of AI. Using Google Photos, I searched the term "landscape" and was intrigued by its interpretation. Alongside traditional landscape photos, the results included images with landscape themes such as pictures of other photographs, books, and phone screenshots.

This project explores the concept of content curation and collection through the overlapping of these diverse landscape images in a collage layout. The layout changes with each page refresh, creating an ever-evolving visual narrative of landscapes. This interactive approach not only tells the story of changing landscapes but also highlights the diverse ways in which landscapes are represented and perceived in our digital lives.
